Menu

Urban Cactus Tacos are made in our premises out of Organic Corn Masa and freshly cooked on a comal (griddle). They are naturally gluten free, Non-GMO, low in fat and sodium, rich in calcium, fiber and magnesium.

They can be filled with a variety of traditional Mexican fillings made with premium quality ingredients.

  • Tacos

    Delicious organic corn tortillas cooked on a griddle on site, stuffed with the filling of your choice. Gluten free

  • Burrito

    Organic flour tortilla, cilantro-poblano rice, pinto beans, organic romaine lettuce and the filling and salsa of your choice.

  • Plate

    Rice, beans, side of lettuce, guacamole and the filling of your choice served with two organic corn tortillas. Gluten free
